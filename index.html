02 / Method

Tradecraft first. Technology second. Judgment always.

My work starts with proof discipline: primary sources, exact quotes, careful caveats, and a clear distinction between what the record says and what the record suggests.

Then I build the machinery around that judgment: monitors, search systems, finance pipelines, briefing engines, and QA loops that help teams move faster without losing the thread.

03 / AI + Research Tooling

I build tools for researchers, by researchers.

AI is useful when it extends the researcher: retrieval, synthesis, entity resolution, document intake, anomaly detection, and first-pass organization. It becomes dangerous when it replaces verification.

The systems I care about keep humans in the judgment loop and make the proof chain visible. The goal is not more content. The goal is better calls under pressure.
